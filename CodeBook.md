Introduction
------------
This codebook is to be used with tidy.txt, a data that summarizes
the average of mean and std values of all 6 activities of all 30
subjects.


Identifiers:
------------
Subject
Actiivity


Activity types
--------------
1 WALKING
2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS
3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
4 SITTING
5 STANDING
6 LAYING


Measurements
------------
Mean_x_av
Mean_y_av
Mean_z_av
STD_x_av
STD_y_av
STD_z_av
